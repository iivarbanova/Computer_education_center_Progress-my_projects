Nessebarcup 2019

U9

The Nessebarcup 2019 International Children's Youth Football Tournament, organized by Sofia Sport, FC Nessebar with the support of
Nessebar Municipality and BFS will be held in two editions on the same dates as in the past years - 20.06-22.06.2019 and 24.08.-26.08.2019.

For the first time in Bulgaria will participate the Finnish Grand HIK Helsinki. In addition to the Finns, the teams of FC Neman Grodno - Belarus,
FC Chadir Lunga Moldova, FC Carpathia, CSS GIURGIU Romania, participated FC Radnicki Pirot, FC PAS FLORINA Greece, eadcoala sportivă
municipului Ceadir-Lunga, FC Oguzsport oraşul Comrat, FC Cahul-2005 oraşul Cahul, as well as teams from Bulgaria.

For more information, visit www.nesebarcup.com
